# CipherQuest Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I restore my purchase?
1. Open CipherQuest
2. Tap "Chapters"
3. Tap any locked chapter
4. Tap "Restore Purchases"

### My progress was lost
Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you deleted the app or got a new device, progress cannot be recovered. However, your purchase can always be restored using the steps above.

### The app is crashing or has a bug
Please email us at support@cipherquestgame.com with:
- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 14)
- iOS version (Settings → General → About)
- What happened and steps to reproduce

### I have feedback or a suggestion
We'd love to hear from you! Email support@cipherquestgame.com
